Summary of Dataset

The source of Data

We use the dataset from Yelp. This dataset is a subset of their businesses, reviews and user data for use in personal, educational and academic purposes, containing over 1.2 million business attributes like hours, parking, availability and ambience. Before combining, our original dataset was comprised of the following 6 JSON files: business.json
- business_id, name, address, city, state, postal code, latitude, longitude, stars, review count, is open, attributes, categories and hours
review.json
- review id, user id, business id, stars, date, text, useful, funny, cool
user.json
- user id, name, review count, yelping since, friends, useful, funny, cool, fans, elite, average stars, compliments
checkin.json
- business id, date
tip.json
- text, date, compliment count, business id, user id
photo.json
- photo id, business id, caption, label
